Dawn of time

Posted on Jul 16, 2022

I was searching for a timeline of world events, and I stumbled upon this: https://www.southampton.ac.uk/~cpd/history.html. It’s a “Timeline of human evolution and cultural development”, as it’s author would describe it, and it’s one of the most useful resources on the topic I’ve seen. I’ve used this script here to extract the relevant tables into CSV format for future processing.
